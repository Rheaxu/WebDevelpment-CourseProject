Preview sheet music and photo

Both guest (unregistered users) and member (registered users) can use this function

1. When to use?

This is for previewing the results of searching and our members collections. You can only see the work, but no detail information, for example, the ownername, comments, create time, etc., about this work.

3. Preview of Sheet Musics

The preview of a photo will provide you a player to play the sheet music. If you are loggedin, you will see a blue "Add to My Collection" button.

By clicking this button, you will be navigated to the Post Sheet Music Page

Otherwise, you will see a grey "Want to add to my Collection? Please Login" button.

This is implemented by a Javascript provided by MuseScore

2. Preview of photos

The preview of a photo will provide you the size of the image. If you are loggedin, you will see a blue "Add to My Gallery" button.

By clicking this button, you will be navigated to the Post Photo Page

Otherwise, you will see a grey "Want to add to my Gallery? Please Login" button.
