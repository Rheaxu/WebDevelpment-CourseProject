Overview Of My Final Project

1. Website Name:

Online Sheet Music Collection and Photo Gallery

2. Main Functions

  • Search: Any users can search, preview sheet musics and photos.
  • Preview: Any users can preview a work
  • Add works: Members can add sheet musics and photos to their own collections and galleries.
  • Like/Unlike: Members can like/unlike other members' sheet musics and photos
  • Comment: Members can comment on any sheet musics and photos.
  • Follow/Unfollow: Members can follow/unfollow any members
  • Profile: Each member has a profile. It lists all information of the member.
  • See All Followers: Members can see all followers of a member
  • See All Followings: Members can see all followings of a member
  • See All Likes: Members can see all works a member likes
  • See All Likers: Members can see all likers of a work
  • See All Collections: Members can see all collections of any members

3. Platform and Database

The project is built up with SQL Server and C# ASP.NET

4. Pages List

The website contains 8 pages that can be accessed by both guests and members and 7 pages that can be accessed only by members.

Public Pages:

  • Home Page
  • Login Page
  • Register Page
  • Search Page
  • All Members' Sheet Music Collection Page
  • All Members' Image Gallery Page
  • Sheet Music Preview page
  • Image Preview Page

Member Pages:

  • Personal Profile Page
  • My Sheet Music Collection Page
  • My Photo Gallery Page
  • Sheet Music Detail Page
  • Photo Detail Page
  • Post Sheet Music Page
  • Post Photo Page

5. Recommended test cases

Notice: "liked sheet music" and "liked photo" in the profile page means the sheet musics/photos in this website the member has clicked "Like" button for. It doens't mean the sheet musics/photos the member has added to his/her collection/gallery.

I. Search and add sheet music to your Sheet Music Collection

Follow the same steps to search and add photo to you Photo Gallery

  1. Click the "login" label on the home page to login (There are three of them: one is in the navigation bar, one is at the top right side of the page and one the under the "welcome" banner)
  2. Click the "Search" on the home page (There are two of them: one is in the navigation bar and one is in the 3D "Sheet Music" book on the home page)
  3. Type the music track name you want to search. For example: christmas
  4. Click "Search Sheet Music"
  5. A table will list all the search result
  6. Choose the one you prefer, for example, "Christmas night"
  7. Click the Orange "Preview" button on the right side, you will be navigated to the preview page of this sheet music.
  8. Click the green play triangle play button at the top of the sheet music to listen to it.
  9. Click the blue "Add to my Sheet Music Collection" button, you will be navigated to the "ADD SHEET MUSIC TO MY COLLECTION" page
  10. The URL is already there. You need to type the Title and Description for it. The maximum character number of the description is 200. There is a character number counter under the description text area.
  11. If there are any mistakes in the information you typed, ther will be a notification with yellow background and red words showing up at the top of the board.
  12. Click "Post" button, you will be navigated to the detail page of this work if you post succeed.
  13. Click the blue "Like" button to like the sheet music you just added. Then the "Likes" count beside the "Like" button will now increase 1.
  14. Now Click the orange "Unlike" button, the "Like" count beside the button will decrease 1.

II. Add comment to a sheet music

Follow the same steps to add comment to a photo

  1. At the bottom of the sheet music detail page, there is a "Add Comment Here" text area.
  2. Type in whatever you want to say, and click "Add Comment" button.
  3. The page will automatically refresh and you will see your comment listed in the comments area.

III. Explore your personal profile

  1. After you log in, click the "My Profile" option in the navigation bar or your username at the top right of the page to go to your personal profile.
  2. You can click the options listed on your profile to explore more.
  3. Expand the "Sheet Music Collection"/"Photo Gallery" option, click the link to view all your sheet music collection / Photo collection.
  4. Expand the "Followers"/"Followings" option, click any of the users to go to their profile.
  5. Expand the "Liked Sheet Music"/ "Liked Photo" option, click any of the sheet musics/photos to view the detail of the work.

IV. See all the members of the website

  1. Click the "Sheet Music" or "Photo" option in the navigation bar.
  2. You will be navigate a page shows all the sheet musics or photos of all the members.
  3. At the bottom of the page, you can see "All our members".
  4. Click any of there names to go to their personal profile.

V. Follow a user

  1. On the personal profile page of the member you are viewing, click the blue "Follow" button to follow the member, then the follower count beside the button will increase 1.
  2. After you followed the member, click the "orange" "Unfollow" button to unfollow the member. Then the follower count beside the button will decrease 1.

VI. View all the sheet musics of our members and add the one you like to your own collection

Follow the same steps for photos. The only difference is that you will see a photo slide on the "All members' photo collection page", and click any of the photo will redirect you to the detail page of the photo.

  1. Click the "Sheet Music" option in the navigation bar. You will be navigated to the "All our members' sheet music page".
  2. Choose the one you want to view, click the blue "detail" button to go to the detail page of this shee music.
  3. Or click the preview button to go to the preview page of this sheet music.

VII. Explore the website as an anonymous user

  1. Click the "logout" button, you will be navigate to the login page.
  2. Click the grey "Go back to home page" button, you will see the "My Profile" option in the navigation bar is dissappered.
  3. Now if you are going to any member's page mentioned in the "Page List" section of this documentation, you will be navigated to the login page first.

6. API

There are 2 APIs used in this project

  • Google Image Search API: For searching Photos
  • MuseScore API: For searching Sheet Musics
