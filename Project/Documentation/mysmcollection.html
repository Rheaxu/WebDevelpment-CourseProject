My Sheet Music Collection Page

Only members have access to this page by logging in.

1. How to access to My Sheet Music Collection Page

There are two ways you can do this:

1. Click the "Go to My Collection" button on the All Members' Sheet Music Collection Page (You will be asked to login first)

2. If you're already logged in, go to your profile, click the Sheet Music Collection link in your profile page

2. What can you see in this page

This page shows a table containing all the sheet musics in your collection. They are ranked by the count of likes.

You can click the "Preview" button to preview it or "Detail" button to see more details about it.

See the documentation of Sheet Music Detail Page
See the documentation of Preview Page

3. Go to the member's profile

If you are not watching your own Sheet Music Collection and very interested in the owner, you can click the underlined name to explore his/her profile.
