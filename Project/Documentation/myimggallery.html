My Photo Gallery Page

Only members have access to this page by logging in.

1. How to access to My Photo Gallery Page

There are two ways you can do this:

1. Click the "Go to My Gallery" button on the All Members' Photo Gallery Page (You will be asked to login first)

2. If you're already logged in, go to your profile, click the Photo Gallery link in your profile page

2. What can you see in this page

This page shows a slide containing all the photos in your gallery. They are ranked by the count of likes. By clicking the left and right edge of the slide, you can change the image for previewing in the slide.

By clicking the thumbnail image in the upper slide, it's larger version will be displayed in the lower slide box.

By clicking the larger image box, you will be navigated to the detail page of this image.

See the documentation of Photo Detail Page

3. Go to the member's profile

If you are not watching your own Sheet Music Collection and very interested in the owner, you can click the underlined name to explore his/her profile.
