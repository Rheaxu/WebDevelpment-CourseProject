    1.Clicking the "Add to my Collection" button on the Sheet Music Preview Page

    2.Clicking the "Add to my Collection" button on the Sheet Music Detail Page

  • Post photo page

    You can use the search provided by this website to get images or get url from any other website.

    1. If you've already got the url of the photo, you can access the post photo page by clicking the "Post New Photo" button on the All Members' Sheet Music Collection Page

2. If you want to search photo on this website, after you go to the preview page you your search result, click the "Add to my Gallery" button on the Photo Preview Page
