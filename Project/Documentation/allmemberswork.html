Sheet Music Collection and Pohoto Gallery of all members

Both guest (unregistered users) and member (registered users) can use this function

Go to All Members' Sheet Music Collection Page
Go to All Members' Photo Gallery Page

1. How to access to these two pages

You can navigate to All Members' Sheet Music Collection Page by clicking the "Sheet Music" button in the navigation bar. See Header Documentation

You can navigate to All Members' Photo Gallery Page by clicking the "Photo" button in the navigation bar. See Header Documentation

2. All Members' Sheet Music page

This part is a table that show all the sheet music collected by our members. They are ranked by their "likes" count.

The owernames name are shown in the table, by clicking their names, you can go to their profile.

In each row, you can click either "Preview" or "Detail" (guests will be asked to login first) to see the sheet music.

See the documentation of Sheet Music Detail Page

Those sheet musics are selected by the following Linq code:

var msmObjList = context.memberSheetMusics.OrderByDescending(p => p.likes).ToList();
popularSMRepeater.DataSource = msmObjList;
popularSMRepeater.DataBind();
memberRepeater.DataSource = context.AuthUsers.ToList();
memberRepeater.DataBind();

3. All Members' Photo Page

This part is a slide that show all the photo collected by our members. They are ranked by their "likes" count.

There is a photo slide on this page, by clicking the arrows on left and right side, you can preview the most popular photos.

By clicking any one photo in the slide, you wil be navigated to the photo detail page of this photo. (Guests will be asked to login first)

See the documentation of Photo Detail Page
var mpObjList = context.memberPhotos.OrderByDescending(p => p.likes).ToList();
popular_repeater.DataSource = mpObjList;
popular_repeater.DataBind();

4. All Our Members List

In both pages, there is a "All Our Members" section at the bottom. It lists all the current members of our website. By clicking their name, you will be navigated to their profile.
